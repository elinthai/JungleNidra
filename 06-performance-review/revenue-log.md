## Layer reference (from jn-monetization)

1. **Ads** — long watch-time compounds even at moderate wellness/sleep RPM.
2. **Affiliate** — sleep-adjacent products (weighted blankets, sound machines, sleep
   masks, trackers) in the description only, never narrated in-video.
3. **Memberships** — ad-free/extended/early-access journeys via YouTube Memberships
   or Patreon.
4. **Licensing** — individual tracks/journeys licensed to sleep/meditation apps
   (Calm-style, Insight Timer, Spotify) — flagged as the standout long-term opportunity
   given the existing script/soundscape library.
5. **Sponsorships** (later stage) — sleep-tech/wellness brand mid-roll mentions, read
   in the anonymous narrator voice.
