# Opening lines — anxiety-relief-waterfall-journey

Welcome. Whatever you were doing a moment ago, you can let it go now. This is a space
to rest — nothing here needs your attention except your own breath. Tonight we're
going to ease the anxious thoughts and settle you gently toward sleep, with the sound
of a hidden waterfall to carry you there. Find a comfortable position, dim the lights
if you can, and put on your headphones if you'd like. There's nowhere else to be right
now.
