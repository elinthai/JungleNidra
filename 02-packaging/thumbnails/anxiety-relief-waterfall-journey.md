# Thumbnail concept — anxiety-relief-waterfall-journey

**VISUAL:** A moonlit waterfall falling into a still, dark pool, soft mist rising off
the water, no human figure anywhere in frame.

**PALETTE (3 max):** deep blue, soft moss green, warm low amber glow (a single soft
light source reflecting on the water — no harsh saturation).

**TEXT:** "ANXIETY RELIEF SLEEP"
Alternate: "FALL ASLEEP TONIGHT"

**FOCAL:** the still pool at the base of the falls — stillness as the visual metaphor
for calming anxiety, with the falling water suggesting release/letting go.
