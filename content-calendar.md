## How to update

- New idea approved → add a row, Stage = `Idea` or `Packaged`/`Scripted` if it came in
  through `/jn-production-line` directly.
- After filming → Stage = `Shooting` (footage back, not yet edited) → `Editing` (in CapCut).
- After `/jn-repurposing` runs → note it, Stage can stay `Editing` (repurposing runs in
  parallel with the long-form edit, per `SOP.md` Stage 4.5).
- After upload → Stage = `Uploaded`, fill in the actual upload date in Status/notes.
- After `/jn-launch-optimization` runs (48+ hrs post-upload) → Stage = `Reviewed`, link
  the diagnosis in `06-performance-review/`.
