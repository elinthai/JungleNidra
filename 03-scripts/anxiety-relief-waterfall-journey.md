TITLE / THEME: Waterfall journey — anxiety relief

TECHNIQUE: Breath settling + body scan into visualization (default combination)

## 1. Welcome & settling

Welcome. Whatever you were doing a moment ago, you can let it go now.

This is a space to rest. Nothing here needs your attention except your own breath.

[PAUSE - 4s]

Tonight, we're going to ease the anxious thoughts and settle you gently toward sleep,
with the sound of a hidden waterfall to carry you there.

Find a comfortable position. Dim the lights if you can. Put on your headphones if
you'd like.

[PAUSE - 4s]

There's nowhere else to be right now. Nothing to solve. Nothing to plan. Just this
moment, and the sound of water.

[PAUSE - 6s]

## 2. Body & breath settling

Let's begin by simply noticing your breath, without changing it.

[PAUSE - 4s]

Breathing in.

[PAUSE - 3s]

And breathing out.

[PAUSE - 4s]

With your next breath in, let it be a little slower.

[PAUSE - 3s]

And as you breathe out, let your shoulders soften and drop.

[PAUSE - 5s]

Notice your jaw. Let it unclench. Let your tongue rest heavy in your mouth.

[PAUSE - 4s]

Notice your forehead. Let it smooth and release.

[PAUSE - 4s]

Feel the weight of your body where you're lying. Heavier with every breath.

[PAUSE - 5s]

Any anxious thought that arrives right now doesn't need to be pushed away. Just let it
pass, like a leaf carried on water.

[PAUSE - 6s]

You are safe. You are supported. There is nothing to hold onto.

[PAUSE - 6s]

## 3. The journey

Imagine yourself walking, slowly, along a quiet path.

[PAUSE - 4s]

The air is warm and soft against your skin. Somewhere ahead, you can hear it — the
gentle rush of falling water.

[PAUSE - 5s]

With each step, the sound grows a little clearer. A little closer.

[PAUSE - 4s]

And then, the path opens, and you see it: a waterfall, falling in slow, silver ribbons
into a still, dark pool below.

[PAUSE - 6s]

The water doesn't rush or hurry. It simply falls, the way it always has, the way it
always will.

[PAUSE - 5s]

You find a smooth stone at the edge of the pool, and you sit.

[PAUSE - 4s]

The mist reaches you gently, cool against your face.

[PAUSE - 5s]

Notice the sound of the water. Let it fill the space where your worries usually sit.

[PAUSE - 6s]

With every falling drop, something in you loosens. Releases. Lets go.

[PAUSE - 6s]

Whatever you've been carrying today, imagine it here now — and let the water carry it
away from you, drop by drop.

[PAUSE - 8s]

You have nowhere to be. Nothing to hold. Just the water, and your breath, and the
stillness of this place.

[PAUSE - 8s]

## 4. Descent / extended quiet

Let yourself sink a little deeper here.

[PAUSE - 6s]

The water keeps falling.

[PAUSE - 8s]

Softly.

[PAUSE - 8s]

Steadily.

[PAUSE - 10s]

You don't need to hold on to anything anymore.

[PAUSE - 10s]

Just the sound of the water now.
